Pumpkin Pie

The one and only pumpkin pie! EAGLE BRAND makes this traditional dessert the perfect ending to a Thanksgiving feast. Garnish as desired. Refrigerate leftovers.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 50 mins
Additional Time: N/A
Total Time: 60 mins
Servings: 8
Yield: 1 9-inch pie

Ingredients

  • Pie crust: Use a store-bought pastry crust or use our top-rated Butter Flaky Pie Crust recipe.
  • Sugar: A little less than one cup of white sugar adds the perfect amount of sweetness.
  • Spices: cinnamon, ginger, and cloves.
  • Salt: Salt enhances the flavors of the other ingredients, adding complexity. Don't skip this step!
  • Eggs: Two eggs add moisture and act as a binding agent, which means they help hold the pumpkin pie filling together.
  • Pumpkin: Of course, you'll need a can of pure pumpkin.
  • Evaporated milk: A can of evaporated milk lends richness and helps thicken the pie filling.

Directions

Step1

Mix the spices in a small bowl.

Step2

Beat the eggs in a large bowl. Stir in the pumpkin and spices, then pour the filling into the unbaked pie crust.

Step3

Bake the pie for about 15 minutes in the preheated oven, then reduce the heat and bake until a knife comes out clean.
